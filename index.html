Section 08

Upgrade Funnel — Calling Flow

Follow this 8-step structure for every upgrade call. Each step builds the next.

📞
Step 1: Greet & Verify
"Good morning Sir/Ma'am, mera naam [Name] hai, main Adda247 se bol raha hoon. Kya aap [Student Name] ji bol rahe hain?"
🔍
Step 2: Identify Current Purchase
Check the CRM for what product they have. Confirm on call: "Aapne humari Test Series/Ebook/Video Course li thi na?"
❓
Step 3: Ask Preparation Status
"Preparation kaise chal rahi hai? Exam ka koi specific target hai kya aapka?"
🎯
Step 4: Identify Gap in Current Product
Establish that what they have is not enough for complete preparation. Use a relevant limitation from the product table.
💎
Step 5: Connect Gap with Mahapack Value
"Jo aapko chahiye — live guidance, tests, recorded videos — yeh sab ek jagah Mahapack mein milta hai."
📊
Step 6: Explain Benefit Comparison
Compare their current product vs Mahapack features. Be specific — mention what they'll additionally get.
⏰
Step 7: Create Urgency or Relevance
Mention exam dates, batch start dates, or limited offer validity to make action urgent.
📣 Campaign Hook — Use at Step 7
"Sir/Ma'am, hum abhi existing users ke liye ek special campaign run kar rahe hain — aur aap iske liye eligible hain."
Use this line right at the urgency step to open the door — then continue with pricing, batch dates, or limited validity. It immediately shifts the tone from "sales push" to "exclusive offer for you."
1
"Sir/Ma'am, hum abhi apne existing users ke liye ek special campaign run kar rahe hain. Aap is campaign ke under Mahapack upgrade kar sakte ho better price par."
2
"Aapke liye ek good news hai — hamare existing students ke liye abhi ek limited campaign chal rahi hai. Isliye aaj hi decision lena aapke liye best time hai."
3
"Sir/Ma'am, main isliye call kar raha tha — hum ek exclusive upgrade campaign run kar rahe hain sirf un students ke liye jo already Adda247 par hain. Aap directly eligible ho."
⚡ Then continue → Batch dates / Offer validity / Payment support
✅
Step 8: Close with Payment / Support
"Kya main aapko payment link bhej doon? Main poori process mein aapki help kar sakta hoon."

What is the Expiry Funnel?

Students whose course, batch, subscription, test series, or Mahapack access has already expired — or is about to expire within 7–30 days.

⏳
Objective

Renew the student by showing loss of access, preparation continuity, updated content, and exam urgency. The tone is reconnect — not pitch.

Section 13

Expiry Student Types

Not all expired students are the same. Identify the type before dialing — it changes everything about your pitch.

Website Active
🟢 Active but Expired
These students still visit the app/website after expiry. Intent is present — they are still exploring. They haven't renewed yet, likely due to price, timing, or waiting for the right reason.
App logins after expiry
Content page visits
Test attempt attempts
Recent browsing activity
Website Inactive
🔴 Inactive & Expired
These students have stopped visiting altogether. They may have lost interest, shifted to a competitor, paused prep, or simply need a strong reconnection trigger to restart.
No app logins
No course activity
No test attempts
No recent engagement
🎯
Trainer Note

For active students: focus on continuity and missing access. For inactive students: first reconnect emotionally, then reactivate the exam goal, then pitch renewal.

Section 14

Expiry Funnel — Calling Flow

An 8-step reconnect and reactivation call structure for all expired students.

👋
Step 1: Greet & Reconnect
"Good morning Sir/Ma'am, main Adda247 se [Name] bol raha hoon. Kaafi time baad baat ho rahi hai — preparation kaise chal rahi hai?"
📋
Step 2: Mention Previous Course
"Aapki [Course Name] ki validity expire ho gayi hai. Main isliye specifically call kar raha hoon."
❓
Step 3: Check Preparation Status
"Abhi preparation continue hai ya koi break liya hai? Exam target still active hai?"
🔍
Step 4: Identify Why Renewal Hasn't Happened
Listen carefully — price, confusion, other platform, lost interest. Identify and address the real block.
📉
Step 5: Show What They're Missing
"Expiry ke baad aap updated classes, live tests, recorded sessions aur study material access nahi kar pa rahe — yeh prep gap create karta hai."
🎯
Step 6: Connect Renewal with Exam Goal
"Aap [Exam Name] ki preparation kar rahe ho — aur exam [date/month] mein hai. Is gap mein updated content aur practice zaroor chahiye."
💎
Step 7: Pitch Renewal / Mahapack
Based on previous product — either pitch a renewal or upgrade to Mahapack for complete access going forward.
📣 Campaign Hook — Use at Step 7 (Expiry)
"Sir/Ma'am, hum abhi existing users ke liye ek special renewal campaign run kar rahe hain — aur aap directly eligible hain."
For expiry students, this line removes the "why are you calling me" resistance. It repositions the call as a benefit notification, not a sales push.
1
"Sir/Ma'am, aapka Mahapack expire hua hai aur hum abhi existing users ke liye special renewal campaign run kar rahe hain. Aap is campaign mein renewal karke complete access restart kar sakte ho."
2
"Main isliye specifically call kar raha tha — aap already hamare student ho, aur hamare existing users ke liye abhi ek limited-time renewal campaign available hai."
3
"Aapke liye ek update tha — hum apne expired students ke liye special reactivation campaign run kar rahe hain. Preparation restart karne ka yeh better time hai."
⚡ Then continue → Renewal plan / Mahapack / Urgency close
✅
Step 8: Close with Urgency / Offer / Support
"Main aapko link bhej deta hoon. Aur agar koi bhi issue ho toh directly mujhe call karo — main personally help karoonga."

Expiry Pitch: Website Active Student

Active But Expired

📞
Expiry Pitch Script — Active Student
"Sir/Ma'am, I noticed ki aapka access expire ho chuka hai, but aap abhi bhi preparation continue kar rahe ho. That means aapka intent strong hai — aur yeh bahut achhi baat hai. Ab agar access renew nahi hota, toh aap updated classes, tests, recorded videos aur study material miss kar sakte ho. Isliye better rahega ki aap renewal ya Mahapack le lo — taaki preparation break na ho aur exam tak complete support mile."

Pitch Logic

Acknowledge their effort first — they are still trying. Then create urgency by highlighting what they are currently missing. Do not start with selling.

✓ Validate "Bilkul, aapka point valid hai"
? Probe "Bas ek cheez samajhna chahta hoon…"
↺ Reframe "Current product helpful hai, but complete prep ke liye…"
🎯 Connect + Pitch "Aapka target selection hai, isliye Mahapack…"
❝ "Mere paas test series hai, wahi enough hai."
Response"Bilkul sir/ma'am, test series practice ke liye very important hai. But test series sirf ye batati hai ki score kya aa raha hai aur weakness kaha hai. Weakness improve karne ke liye concept classes, recorded lectures aur study material bhi chahiye. Mahapack mein test series ke saath complete learning support milta hai."
↺ Reframe"Test series result dikhata hai, Mahapack result improve karne ka system deta hai."

Response"Sir/ma'am, score analysis problem batata hai, solution nahi deta. Agar Quant mein percentage weak hai, analysis dikha dega ki weak hai — but strong karne ke liye class, explanation aur revision material chahiye. Mahapack isi gap ko cover karta hai."
↺ Reframe"Analysis tells the problem, Mahapack gives the solution."
